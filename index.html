How do I book a therapy session?

You can book a therapy session directly through our website by browsing our therapists and selecting an available time slot that works for you. Alternatively, you can call our support line for assistance with booking.

Are online sessions available?

Yes! We offer both in-person and online therapy sessions. Many of our clients prefer the convenience of online sessions, which are conducted through our secure video platform.

What's the difference between therapy and coaching?

Therapy typically focuses on diagnosing and treating mental health conditions, while coaching is more focused on personal development and achieving specific goals. Our professionals can help you determine which approach is right for your needs.

Can I switch therapists if I don't feel the right connection?

Absolutely. Finding the right therapist is crucial for effective treatment. We encourage you to try different therapists until you find someone you feel comfortable with, and our team can help guide you in this process.

Do I need a subscription to access services?

No subscription is required. You can book individual sessions as needed, though we do offer packages for those who want to commit to ongoing care at a discounted rate.

Is my information and session history kept confidential?

Yes, confidentiality is our top priority. All your information and session details are protected under strict privacy laws and our secure systems. We never share your information without your explicit consent.
